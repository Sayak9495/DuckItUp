DuckItUp: A DuckDuckGo Extension (1.3)

DuckItUp is a DuckDuckGo Extension which integrates few productivity features to DuckDuckGo like:

  • Dedicated Search Google button. [Redirects to google with the same search query]
  • Movie & TV-Series Ratings info gets integrated when searched for a movie. [like year of release, genre, run-time, IMDb/Rotten-Tomato/Metacritic ratings]
  • Shows Movie/Series Info even when DuckDuckGo doesn't detect the query as a valid movie/series. [Suffix such queries with movie/series and DuckItUp will integrate the movie/series info into the view. Ex- Cosmos series].
